THE COSMIC FRAMEWORK

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the COSMIC Framework?

The COSMIC Framework is a comprehensive theory proposing that information processing is fundamental to physical reality. It builds on established physics (quantum mechanics, thermodynamics, information theory) to explore whether information principles can explain patterns we currently treat as mysteries.

How is this different from string theory or other theories of everything?

Unlike string theory which proposes new fundamental entities, the COSMIC Framework uses established physics and asks whether information processing is fundamental. It makes specific, testable predictions that can be validated or falsified through experimentation.

What does "information" mean in physics?

In physics, information isn't about meaning or knowledge—it measures distinguishable states. A bit can be 0 or 1 (two states). Landauer's principle proved that processing this information costs measurable energy, making information physical, not abstract.

Is this theory proven?

No. Parts are established (Landauer's principle, quantum error correction). Parts are testable predictions. Parts will likely be wrong. That's why it's a framework needing validation, not a theory claiming certainty. All predictions can be tested experimentally.
