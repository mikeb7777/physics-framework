HOW TO ADD A NEW BLOG POST (15 MINUTES PER POST)
==========================================================

OVERVIEW:
For each new article, you'll:
1. Copy the template file
2. Fill in your content
3. Add metadata to blog-data.json
4. Upload both files to GitHub

DETAILED WALKTHROUGH:

STEP 1: Prepare Your Article
-----------------------------
Before you start, have ready:
‚ñ° Article title
‚ñ° Your content (1,000-3,000 words recommended)
‚ñ° Category (theory, consciousness, experiments, philosophy, mathematics)
‚ñ° Brief excerpt (1-2 sentences)
‚ñ° Estimated read time (approx. 200 words = 1 minute)
‚ñ° Tags (3-5 relevant keywords)
‚ñ° Icon/emoji (optional, for visual appeal)

STEP 2: Create URL Slug
------------------------
Convert your title to a URL-friendly slug:

Example: "Information as the Foundation of Reality"
Becomes: "information-foundation-reality"

Rules for slugs:
- All lowercase
- Replace spaces with hyphens
- Remove special characters
- Keep it short but descriptive

STEP 3: Copy and Customize the Template
---------------------------------------
1. Download blog-post-template.html
2. Save it with your slug name: blog-post-[SLUG].html
   Example: blog-post-information-foundation-reality.html

3. Open in a text editor and replace these placeholders:

In the <head> section:
   [ARTICLE EXCERPT] ‚Üí Your 1-2 sentence excerpt
   [AUTHOR NAME] ‚Üí Your name
   [ARTICLE TITLE] ‚Üí Your article title

In the article header:
   [CATEGORY] ‚Üí theory, consciousness, etc.
   [ARTICLE TITLE] ‚Üí Your title
   [ICON] ‚Üí Choose an emoji (üåå üß† ‚öõÔ∏è üî¨ üí≠ üìê)
   [AUTHOR NAME] ‚Üí Your name
   [DATE] ‚Üí October 24, 2025 (or your publish date)
   [READ TIME] ‚Üí Estimated minutes

In the author box:
   [AUTHOR BIO] ‚Üí Brief bio (1-2 sentences)

In the article content section:
   Replace ALL placeholder text with your actual article content

STEP 4: Write Your Article Content
----------------------------------
In the <article class="article-content"> section:

Structure your article with:
- <h2> for major sections
- <h3> for subsections
- <p> for paragraphs
- <blockquote> for important quotes
- <div class="callout"> for key insights
- <ul> or <ol> for lists
- <strong> for emphasis
- <em> for secondary emphasis

EXAMPLE STRUCTURE:
```html
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>Your opening paragraph...</p>

<h2>The Problem</h2>
<p>Describe the issue or question...</p>

<blockquote>
"A relevant quote or key statement"
</blockquote>

<h2>The Solution</h2>
<p>Present your ideas...</p>

<div class="callout">
    <div class="callout-title">üí° Key Insight</div>
    <p>Your important takeaway...</p>
</div>

<h2>Implications</h2>
<p>What does this mean?...</p>

<h2>Conclusion</h2>
<p>Summarize and inspire further thought...</p>
```

STEP 5: Update Tags Section
---------------------------
Replace the example tags with your relevant tags:
```html
<a href="#" class="tag">#your-tag-1</a>
<a href="#" class="tag">#your-tag-2</a>
<a href="#" class="tag">#your-tag-3</a>
```

STEP 6: Add to blog-data.json
-----------------------------
1. Open blog-data.json in GitHub
2. Click pencil icon (Edit)
3. Add your post to the "posts" array:


- Use YYYY-MM-DD format for dates
- Set featured: true for only ONE post at a time
- Match the slug exactly to your filename

4. Click "Commit changes"

==========================================================
COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS
==========================================================

PROBLEM: Article not showing on blog page
SOLUTION:
1. Check blog-data.json for syntax errors (use jsonlint.com)
2. Verify "status" is "published"
3. Clear browser cache
4. Check file uploaded correctly

PROBLEM: Formatting looks wrong
SOLUTION:
1. Validate HTML (use validator.w3.org)
2. Check for unclosed tags
3. Ensure CSS classes match template
4. Test in different browsers

PROBLEM: Featured post not displaying
SOLUTION:
1. Ensure only ONE post has featured: true
2. Check blog-data.json syntax
3. Refresh cache
4. Verify JavaScript is loading

PROBLEM: Search not working
SOLUTION:
1. Check JavaScript console for errors
2. Verify blog-data.json is accessible
3. Clear cache and hard refresh

PROBLEM: Low member submissions
SOLUTION:
1. Promote submission form more
2. Email subscribers directly
3. Offer incentives (contributor credit, etc.)
4. Make guidelines clearer
5. Share examples of good submissions
