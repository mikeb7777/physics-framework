About the Predictions

What predictions have been validated?
Three major predictions have been validated: (1) Dark energy evolution showing time-varying behavior (DESI, January 2025), (2) Quantum error correction with exponential error suppression (Google's Willow chip, December 2024), and (3) Early galaxy formation patterns (JWST, ongoing 2024).
How were the predictions documented before validation?
All predictions are documented in versioned releases with publication dates. Version 1.0 (March 2024) contained the initial predictions, well before the experimental validations in late 2024 and early 2025. Complete version history is available on our downloads page.

Technical Questions

What mathematical framework does The Big TOE use?
The COSMIC Framework builds on established physics principles including quantum field theory, general relativity, and information theory. It introduces novel mathematical structures for understanding the relationship between quantum mechanics, spacetime, and consciousness. Complete derivations are provided in the book.
How does the framework address the measurement problem?
The framework proposes a specific mechanism for wavefunction collapse based on information-theoretic principles. This leads to testable predictions about quantum decoherence rates and measurement outcomes that can be experimentally verified.
Is the framework compatible with string theory?
The COSMIC Framework is formulated independently but may have points of contact with string theory and other unified theories. The key difference is our emphasis on falsifiable, near-term predictions rather than mathematical elegance alone.
