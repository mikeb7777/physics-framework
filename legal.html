About The Big TOE & The COSMIC Framework

The COSMIC Framework is a comprehensive theoretical framework that proposes information as the fundamental substrate of reality, from which all physical phenomena emerge. Developed by Michael K. Baines, this framework attempts to unify quantum mechanics, relativity, consciousness studies, and cosmology into a coherent Theory of Everything.

What is "The Big TOE"?

"The Big TOE" refers to "Theory of Everything," representing an ambitious attempt to explain the fundamental nature of reality through an information-theoretic lens. The COSMIC Framework proposes testable predictions and seeks to bridge the gap between physics, consciousness, and the nature of reality itself.

About the Author

Michael K. Baines is an independent researcher dedicated to exploring fundamental questions about the nature of reality. This work represents years of synthesis across multiple disciplines, offered freely to encourage discussion, critique, and advancement of these ideas.

Mission

Our mission is to make theoretical physics and consciousness research accessible to everyone, fostering meaningful dialogue and advancing our collective understanding of reality. By keeping this book freely available, we remove barriers to knowledge and encourage global participation in these important questions.
