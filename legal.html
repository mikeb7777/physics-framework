Information about The COSMIC Framework

About Us

The COSMIC Framework represents a groundbreaking approach to understanding the fundamental nature of reality. Developed by Michael K. Baines, this theoretical framework proposes that information is not merely a description of reality, but the fundamental substrate from which all physical phenomena emerge.

Our Mission

To advance our understanding of the universe through rigorous scientific inquiry, collaborative research, and open discussion. We believe that the deepest truths about reality emerge from the intersection of physics, information theory, and consciousness studies.

The Framework

The COSMIC Framework builds upon established physics while proposing novel connections between:

  • Information theory and thermodynamics
  • Quantum mechanics and consciousness
  • Gravitational phenomena and information density
  • Mathematical constants and physical reality

Research Approach

We are committed to transparent, reproducible research. All methodologies, data analysis protocols, and preliminary findings are made available for independent verification and peer review.

Community

Join our growing community of researchers, physicists, philosophers, and curious minds exploring these profound questions about the nature of reality.
