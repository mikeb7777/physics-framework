Support Open Research

Your contributions enable us to keep knowledge freely accessible while advancing theoretical physics research.

We believe in letting you determine the value of this work.

If you didn't find value in the book, please don't feel obligated to contribute. If you found it valuable, insightful, or useful in your own research, we welcome your support.

Your contribution helps us continue this research and keeps all our work freely accessible to everyone.

Academic and theoretical physics books of similar scope typically sell for $40-$150 in the commercial market.

We've made this work freely available because we believe financial circumstances shouldn't be a barrier to accessing scientific knowledge.

The amount you contribute is entirely up to you. Contribute what feels right based on the value you received.
