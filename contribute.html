Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible? ‚ñº

Currently, Ic¬≤ Research Institute operates as an independent research platform, not a registered 501(c)(3) organization. Therefore, donations are not tax-deductible at this time. We maintain this status to ensure complete independence and avoid institutional pressures.

How do I know my money is being used effectively? ‚ñº

We publish quarterly financial reports detailing every dollar received and spent. All financial records are publicly available, and we maintain a commitment to radical transparency. You can review our financial statements anytime on our website.

Can I contribute in ways other than money? ‚ñº

Absolutely! We welcome research collaborations, technical contributions, peer review, documentation improvements, and community engagement. Visit our GitHub or contact us directly to discuss how you can contribute your expertise.

Will you ever introduce paywalls or restrict access? ‚ñº

Never. Our commitment to open science is absolute. All research, data, and publications will always remain freely accessible to everyone, everywhere. This is a core value that will never change, regardless of funding challenges.

Can I set up a recurring donation? ‚ñº

Yes! PayPal allows you to set up monthly recurring donations. This provides stable, predictable funding that helps us plan long-term research initiatives. You can modify or cancel recurring donations anytime through your PayPal account.
