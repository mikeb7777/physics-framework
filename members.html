About Membership

The IcÂ² Research Institute is a collaborative workspace of independent researchers working to advance theoretical physics and consciousness studies. We welcome three types of participation:

Research Members

  • Retain 100% of the rights to your work - You own everything you create
  • Choose your publication approach - Publish under the institute's auspices or independently in your own name
  • Collaborate with independent researchers - Join project groups or start your own
  • Access collaborative resources - Share knowledge and resources with fellow members
  • Contribute to cutting-edge research - Work on projects aligned with your interests and expertise

Support Members

  • Provide essential services - Accounting, graphic arts, web design, social media marketing, legal, and other support functions
  • Enable research excellence - Help researchers focus on science by handling operational needs
  • Retain full rights - Own your creative work and contributions
  • Flexible engagement - Work on projects or institute-wide needs as available

Observers

  • Complete transparency - Read-only access to project activities
  • Stay informed - Receive updates on research progress
  • Participate in discussions - Comment when comments are open
  • Open to all site members - Anyone can become an observer in any project group
